Instructions

In this HIT you will be matched with another MTurker.
Each round, both of you start with 140 points.

In each round, you both make a choice:

Keep your 140 points;
or transfer your 140 points to the other person, which will be doubled so they get 280 points.

This means that:

...if you both choose to transfer, you each get 280 points.
...if neither of you transfer, you each get 140 points.

But:

...if you transfer your 140 points while the other person does not, you get 0 points while the other person gets 420 points.
...if you do not transfer while the other person transfers 140 points, you get 420 points while the other person gets 0 points.

So even though, in a round:

...you both earn more if you both transfer than if you both keep;
...no matter what the other person does, you earn more by keeping your 140 points.


Only one of your decisions will be used to calculate your bonus.
So, please treat each decision as if it will be the one selected for payment.
